THE OLD MAN AND THE SEA

Ernest Hemingway

⁂

The Old and the sea is a novella written

by the american author Ernest Hermingway in

1951 in Cayo Blanco(cuba), and publish in 1952.

It was the last major work of fiction

written by Hemingway that was published

during this lifetime. One of the most famous work,

it tell the story of Santiago, an giant

marlin far out in the Gulf Stream off the coast of cuba.

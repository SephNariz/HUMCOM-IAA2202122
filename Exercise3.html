Chicken Pesto

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. Toasted Cashew
  • 0.5 pc Lemon
  • 0.15 tsp. Salt
  • 0.25 tsp. Ground Pepper
  • 1 package Fresh Milk
  • 1 tsp. Sugar
  • 3 tbsp. Grated Parmesan
  • 0.25 kg Chicken Thigh
  • 1 sachet MAGGI Magic Sarap 8g
  • 0.25 tsp. Ground Pepper
  • 1 tbsp. Vegetable Oil
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 0.25 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 cup Basil
  • 0.5 cup All Purpose Cream
  • 0.25 kg Spaghetti

Instruction

  1. Cook garlic and olive oil in a small pot until soft. Transfer in a blender. Add basil, cashew, lemon zest and juice. Season with salt, pepper, sugar and cheese. Purée until smooth. Transfer in an airtight container and store in the refrigerator.
  2. Season chicken with ½ sachet of MAGIC SARAP and 1/8 tsp. of pepper. Sauté chicken in oil until golden brown. Remove from the pan, coat with 2 tbsp. of the pesto and set aside.
  3. Pour remaining pesto in the same pan. Pour Fresh Milk and All Purpose Cream. Simmer and season with remaining ½ sachet of magic sarap and pepper.
  4. Boil pasta according to package direction.
  5. Strain spaghetti, toss in the sauce and Transfer on a serving plate. Cut chicken into serving pieces, place on top of pasta and serve.
